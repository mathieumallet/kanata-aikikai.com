Welcome to the Kanata Aikikai
The Kanata Aikikai is an Aikido Dojo located in the Kanata region. Founded in 1985 by David Yates Sensei, it was the first martial school estalished in the city of Kanata.

Visitors are always welcome!

The Kanata Aikikai is celebrating its 40th Anniversary with a seminar on February 22nd 2025. Click here for more details.

Health and Safety
Rowans Law: While Aikido is not one of the identified sports covered by Rowan's law (Concussions), information can be found on the Ontario Aikido Federations website, including documents for the return to practice following a concussion.

First Aid/CPR: As a certified Group Fitness, AquaFit and Spinning Instructor, as well as a Personal Trainer, Yates Sensei must complete his First Aid and CPR training annually. There is also an AED (Automated External Defibrillator) onsite at all times.
